Never Late Salaryman

You are Yamada, an average Japanese salaryman who uses a grappling hook he discovered to move around the city to get to work on time.
Avoid obstacles, bypass security guards, collect key cards, and perform feets of agility to get through each level before the clock reaches zero.

Controls

  • A/D - Move left/right
  • K - Aim/fire grappling hook
  • L - Detatch grappling hook
  • K + W - Aim up
  • K + A - Aim left
  • K + D - Aim right
